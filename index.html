about us

Visiting the dentist can be a daunting experience, we know life can be stressful enough without having to worry about the dentist.

That's why our practice is different. We have created a warm and friendly environment where our team of passionate professionals will show you how easy it can be to care for your teeth. We strive to work at your pace-small step at a time if need be ensuring you get honest advice and quality care.

We want everyone to feel welcome at our clinic. We provide NHS and private treatment options for everybody. So please do get in touch for a breath of fresh air.
