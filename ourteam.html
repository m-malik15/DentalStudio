OUR TEAM

DENTAL • ORTHODONTICS • FACIAL REJUVENATION

The Dental Studios team comprise of highly qualified, trained, collaborative staff focused on delivering both routine and specialized oral care while ensuring patient safety and comfort. Led by dentists, dental surgeons and dental therapists, the team includes dental nurses who assist chair-side, prepare materials, and maintain strict sterilization standards. This multidisciplinary team works together to improve patient oral health, often operating under high-pressure conditions that require excellent communication, empathy, and strict adherence to hygiene protocols.
